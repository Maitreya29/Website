About Us - How did it all start?

It all started on a sunny morning when Rudra Sharma drafted Technolympics which was not a copy of (c)ync and was very happy about it. He caught his breath and planned out the whole event secretly like a ninja, but to his utter dissapointment, everybody got to know about the great event. Fir kya tha... better names for the event like Spectre and Hive started getting brainstormed but with due decisions of our alumnis as well as the members, we settled on the name Technocon. Oh shiz, I almost forgot this is not Technocon. Basically the story for Rudra's Insitute is very short.. After we had great leadership and guidance, we decided to form an instiute for the legends, period.
